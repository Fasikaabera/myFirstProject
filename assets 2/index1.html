Promoting Teaching

Promotion: good practice benchmarks

A brief resource to enable universities to:

  • Identify gaps and good practice in university promotion processes
  • Decide on aspects of promotin processes
  • Improve alignment between policy and practice
  • Assess what evidence about teaching is counted for promotion

Teaching: making evidence count

A ground-breaking framework which will support universities to:

  • Clarify the scope and diversity of teaching related activity
  • Acknowledge the growing emphasis on term practice and leadership in teaching
  • Raise awareness of influence and impact in teaching leadership
  • Expand the arrays and source of evidence considered for promotion especially peer review
  • Support academics in gathering teaching evidence for awards,grants and promotion
  • Align HR processes- recruitment, induction, probation, career development and promotion

Quality: benchmarking guide

A comprehensive step-by step guide and templates which show universities how to benchmark their promotion practices for improved recognition of teaching:

  • Map academic promotion to enhance awareness within the university of the university of big picture
  • Conduct a self-review against the good practice benchmarks
  • Survey or interview staff on perceptions of teaching in academic promotion
  • collect statics on Promotion committe outcomes
  • Engage in cross-institutional review or a change academy targeting promotion
  • Recognise and share good practice in university promotion processes
  • Facilitate activities in promotion preparation workshops
